About The Conference

The CNLDS-2023 is organized in honor of the late Prof. K. Porsezian on his 60th Birthday. The conference aims to provide a platform for young students, experts, researchers, and other stakeholders to share their knowledge, insights, and experiences in the broad area of nonlinear dynamics and complex systems in STEM. The event facilitates -

  • A platform for research Collaboration & Networking Opportunities
  • To identify emerging trends, gaps, and opportunities for further research
  • Enables knowledge exchange, capacity building, and training opportunities
  • To foster a multi & trans-disciplinary approach to solving complex problems
  • To promote diversity, inclusion, and equity in STEM
  • Location IIT Hyderabad
  • Date & Time 05-07, June 2023
  • Speakers 50 Professionals
  • Seats 100 People

Why You Should Join?

The conference features plenary talks, keynote lectures, invited talks, and other contributory papers in the form of oral and poster presentations. Authors are encouraged to submit a one-page abstract of the recent work on the topics related to the scope of the conference.
